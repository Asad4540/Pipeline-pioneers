Privacy Policy - Pipeline Pioneers

Pipline Pioneers is committed to protecting your privacy as you navigate through our website. Please read our policy to understand how your personal information will be used while on our site and how we work to ensure your online experience is secure.

Information Collection and Use

Pipline Pioneers collects information in several ways and at various points on our website. We may also act as an agent to collect data for some of our partners. When you fill out subscription or contact forms, we may ask for personal data (such as your name, title, company, address, telephone, email, and any other demographic or business-related details). This information is securely passed to our partner or their agent for fulfillment.

Pipline Pioneers collects opt-in email addresses that may be used for periodic messages, including announcements of updates to the website and promotional offers. You will have the option to opt-in for none, one, or both types of messages. Users who choose to receive only Pipeline Pioneers announcements will never have their email addresses sold, shared, or rented. Businesses may purchase the right to send promotional messages to users who have opted to receive third-party communications. However, these emails will be sent by Pipeline Pioneers, and your email address will never be shared with the third party. In all cases, email subscribers will have the option to opt-out at any time.

Data Security

We take all necessary steps to ensure that your personal information is protected. However, we recommend that you use secure passwords and not share sensitive details unless you're sure about the site's security.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Pipeline Pioneers uses cookies to improve your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and provide personalized content and ads. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that allow us to recognize you when you return to our website. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, but doing so may affect certain features of the website.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to any personal data we hold about you, as well as to correct or delete it. If you wish to exercise these rights or have any concerns regarding your privacy, please contact us via the contact details provided on our website.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Pipeline Pioneers reserves the right to update or modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically.
